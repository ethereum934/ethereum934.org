Ethereum 9¾

You can enter into the magical world by depositing ERC20 tokens to Ethereum 9¾.
And then, use Mimblewimble spells to send your tokens privately.
If you submit a valid proof, you can withdraw your tokens back to the muggle world.

Introduction

Using crypto money in our daily lives is still a future until we split the bill by using DAI and Metamask rather than using Venmo. One of the causes of this situation is the privacy problem; especially, as many people start to use ENS, we are experiencing the privacy-less world. Representatively, through Etherscan, we can quickly check an ENS owner’s ETH balance, where the money comes from, or even how much is lent from MakerDAO. It makes us hesitate to share ENS and send crypto money to each other. To solve this problem, Ethereum 9¾ comes up as a protocol to send ERC20 privately in a cost-effective way using Mimblewimble and zk-SNARKs. Ethereum 9¾ is a construction of Mimblewimble, Pedersen hash, Merkle Mountain Range(MMR), and (optimistic) roll-up with zk-SNARKs. Using both concepts of ZCash’s commitment-nullifier scheme and Mimblewimble protocol together on Ethereum, it hides the transaction details and the token flow while guaranteeing no money is printed out of thin air. Also, Pedersen Hash and Merkle Mountain Range reduce the gas costs and allow rolling up 64 items at once. Through this construction, it achieves a cost effective private transaction by the optimistic roll-up which supports a deterministic fraud-proof without any data availability problem. Moreover, as relayers can aggregate transactions and accept fees in ERC20 by the Mimblewimble protocol, it is expected to see new DAOs and De-Fi products using Ethereum 9¾.
